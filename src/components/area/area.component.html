Area Calculation

Note:

The area is calculated using the Shoelace formula, also known as Gauss's area formula.

How the Shoelace formula works:
  1. Write down the coordinates of the vertices of the polygon in clockwise or counterclockwise order.
  2. Add the product of the x-coordinate of each vertex and the y-coordinate of the next vertex.
  3. Subtract the product of the y-coordinate of each vertex and the x-coordinate of the next vertex.
  4. Divide the result by 2 to get the area.
